Mild Takes (Part 1?)

May 29, 2019

I'm writing this in 10 minutes because I need something on here. I think about these a lot, so I'll write them down. I may or may not explain/defend these in a later post.

1. Stephen Curry is the most impactful basketball player of all time.

2. As of 2019, Kawhi Leonard is the best player in the world.

3. The Sixers are better off not using Ben Simmons as a point guard.

4. The Rockets need to fire Mike D'Antoni for any chance at a championship.

5. LeBron and AD together beat the Warriors.

6. Devin Booker is a future MVP-caliber player.

7. The most apt modern comparison for Zion Williamson is (in-shape) Draymond Green.

8. RJ Barrett doesn't meet expectations as a rookie, makes 2nd team.

9. Warriors in 5 - bye KD.
