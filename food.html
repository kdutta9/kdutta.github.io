La Val's Pizza (Berkeley, CA)

May 22, 2019

I had this like two Fridays ago coming back from an EE review session (shoutout HKN) and I was mentally and physically tired, just craving some pasta. I ordered the broccoli and chicken penne, which overall, I'd say was average. Not sure if La Val's emphasizes their pasta. It was tasty, but I think the benchmark for me really enjoying a meal is for me feeling like I can't replicate it myself. However, the ambiance of the place is great. I had this right after a Warriors playoff game and the TVs and pool table were a godsend. In the grand scheme, I think I put Gypsy's and Pasta Bene ahead for pasta in Berk, but this place is solid for a cheap meal with a lot of carbs. I'll try the pizza next time for sure.
